July the Forth Festivities

Renae Hadfield, Soda Springs Journal, July 3, 2019

Come join our little town for a good time on the fourth of July. There will be many events going on through out the day starting with the annual "Put a Spring in Your Step 5K that starts at 5a.m. The starting line is at Mellon's on us. Let's not let Mike Willow show us all up, the winner of last year's event.

From there hit us up for some fine country breakfast at the Soda pavilion in Snow Park. There will be games and music all day so come join.

Then when the night sky has come upon us we will be showing of our colors, fireworks at the North end. Everyone remember to be safe and most importantly to have fun.Continue to full article
