Little Fish Haven, Idaho, is a place to enjoy.

By Renae Hadfield, July 3, 2019 Idaho State Journal

FISH HAVEN, Idaho — It's a small town full of beauty. It has the magnificent peaks of the mountains in the background and the beauty of the lake in the valley.

It is a place to find enjoyment all year round. During the winter you have plenty of space to feel the wind rush by as you snow mobile. The summer is a great time to hit the lake for a quick dip in the water or hop on those water skies. There is something for everyone.
