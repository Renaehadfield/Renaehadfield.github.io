Thousands of Idaho Residents Rattled by More Than 60 Earthquakes

By Pam WrightSeptember 04 2017 05:30 AM EDTweather.com

One of the strongest earthquakes to hit Idaho in years struck the southeastern region of the state Saturday night, followed by more than 60 smaller quakes throughout the night and into Saturday.

According to the Idaho State Journal, residents in Caribou County were startled by the 5.3-magnitude earthquake that hit shortly before 6 p.m local time Saturday. No structural damage or injuries were reported.
